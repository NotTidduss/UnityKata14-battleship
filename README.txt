Unity Kata #14 - Battleship

What is Battleship?
	- 2 Player game
	- Guessing game
	- Pencil & Paper game
	- Phase 1: Players place ships on grids.
	- Phase 2: Players try to hit other player's ships by guessing their position.
	- The winner is whoever guesses all the ships first.
